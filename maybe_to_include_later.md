### Sequence of Learning Needs for New Children

**Sequence of learning needs of a new child progressing in the daycare:**
- Becoming familiar and feeling safe with the caregivers, space and other children
- Getting comfortable with the activities on the timetable, knowing how to behave and have fun confidently. Also getting comfortable with routine personal-care like meals, toileting, handwashing, and nap.
- Learning to ask for assistance with items and other children. Exploring all the daycare spaces. Testing the boundaries of the daycare rules in different sessions.
- Developing and chasing personal interests and favorite/preferred activities. Shifting focus from exploring the new environment and community, to investigating particular details or changes, or questing after particular interests.
