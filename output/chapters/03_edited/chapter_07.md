## 7.1 Dormitory Rules and Regulations

### 7.1.1 Daily Schedule

- Staff must wake up by 5:00 AM every day.
- Dormitory duties must be completed by 6:00 AM to ensure the daycare is ready for children by opening time.

### 7.1.2 Attendance and Punctuality

- Staff must be in the daycare by 9:00 PM each evening and are not permitted to leave the daycare past 9:00 PM, unless otherwise instructed by management.

### 7.1.3 Dress Code

- Staff must wear the provided uniforms during working hours.

### 7.1.4 Professional Conduct

- Maintain a friendly and respectful attitude towards children, parents, and fellow staff members at all times.
- Avoid using inappropriate language or behavior in the daycare premises.

### 7.1.5 Safety and Security

- Follow emergency procedures as outlined in the daycare's safety protocols.
- Maintain cleanliness in all dormitory rooms including kitchen, toilets, bathrooms and outside veranda.
- Follow hygiene practices, including regular handwashing, to prevent the spread of germs.
- Caregivers are not permitted to open the gate for unfamiliar guests or anyone who is not recognized.
- All visitors must:
  - Provide identification and state their purpose of visit
  - Wait for management or a supervisor to authorize entry
- These rules help maintain a secure environment for every child in our care.

### 7.1.6 Responsibilities

- Perform assigned duties efficiently and effectively.
- Cooperate with other staff members to ensure smooth operation of daily activities.
- The kitchen should be closed by 6:00 AM and the keys kept by the dormitory manager.
- An alternate copy of the kitchen-key and night-padlock should be kept by the daycare manager, separate from the main daycare keyring.

### 7.1.7 Use of Facilities

- Respect the dormitory and daycare facilities.
- Report any maintenance issues promptly to the management.

### 7.1.8 Gas Refill Cost Sharing

- Staff members are required to share the cost of gas refills equally depending on the number of staff members using it.
- You should not use the daycare's gas stove.

### 7.1.9 Confidentiality

- Maintain confidentiality regarding children and families' personal information.
- Respect one another's personal boundaries.
